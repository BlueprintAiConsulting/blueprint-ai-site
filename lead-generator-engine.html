Tiers

Starter / Standard / Premium

Starter

  • Ads: 2 ad creatives per month (1 short video + 1 image OR 2 videos), Meta campaign setup + management, weekly optimization
  • Social: 6 posts per month (2 per week)
  • SEO: Google Business Profile tune-up (once), on-page SEO for 1 core service page (once)
  • Reporting: monthly recap (leads, cost per lead, what we are changing)
  • Note: client pays ad spend directly to Meta

Standard

  • Ads: 4 ad creatives per month (2 videos + 2 images OR 4 videos), Meta campaign management, optimization 2x per week, retargeting setup (site visitors + engagement)
  • Social: 12 posts per month (3 per week)
  • SEO: Google Business Profile optimization + posting plan, on-page SEO for 3 service pages (initial), technical baseline (speed/indexing/sitemap)
  • Reporting: monthly report + next-month plan

Premium

  • Ads: 8 ad creatives per month (video-first), Meta campaign management, optimization 3x per week, advanced retargeting (site + lead form + video views), A/B testing cadence
  • Social: 20 posts per month (5 per week)
  • SEO: Google Business Profile + review engine, on-page SEO for 5 to 8 pages (initial), 2 local SEO content posts per month (FAQ/service-area), optional citation cleanup as add-on
  • Reporting: bi-weekly KPI check-in + monthly strategy update
