What we fix

  • Leads falling through the cracks

The Blueprint method

  • 1) Map it: We map your real workflow (not the ideal one).
  • 2) Design it: We design the simplest system that solves the bottleneck.
  • 3) Build it: We implement it inside your tools (or deploy a custom app if needed).
  • 4) Hand it off: We train you, document it, and make it easy to run.

FAQ

Quick answers before you reach out.

Do I need to be “techy” for this to work?

No. The goal is a system you can run daily. I build for owners and crews, then train you in plain language.

What tools do you build in?

Whatever fits your workflow. In many cases we use simple tools first. If you need a custom app, we deploy that too.

How fast can we ship a first version?

Quick Win Builds are designed to ship fast. Larger buildouts depend on scope, but the goal is always an early working version.

What does “custom app” mean?

A lightweight app tailored to your process—pipeline, notes, follow-up, job tracking, dashboards—built so your team actually uses it.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. You’ll also get documentation and training so you’re not dependent on me for basic changes.

What should I start with?

If you’re unsure, start with the Free AI Blueprint or a Quick Win Build focused on your biggest bottleneck (often lead capture or follow-up).
